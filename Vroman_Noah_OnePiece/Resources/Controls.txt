---------- Controls ----------
ARROW LEFT/RIGHT: Move left/right 
SPACEBAR: Jump / Press Start
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
ATTACKS:
S: Punch 
D: Grab sideways
ARROW UP + D: Grab up
ARROW UP + ARROW RIGHT/LEFT + D: Grab diagonal 
2 * Press S: Double Punch
3 * Press S: Triple Punch 
S in air: Air attack
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
SOUND:
O: Increase Volume
L: Decrease Volume
M or , : Mute/Unmute
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
